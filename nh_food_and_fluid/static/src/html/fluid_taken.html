Be ALERT to increased fluid intake needs when not eating or if eating poorly or if obese or if there are fluid losses eg: vomiting, diarrhoea, pyrexia, sweating or wound exudate.

NOTE Children: For 5 to 6 year olds, 1000ml is adequate fluid intake. For 7 to 12 year olds, 1200ml is adequate fluid intake.

State approximate measures, eg. Fluids: 1 average cup = 180ml, 1 average mug = 250ml (cup/mug measures may vary). Food: dinner plate eaten or 1 slice of toast.

  • 0 - 1500ml or more; adequate intake
  • 1 - If 1200 ml or more, but less than 1500ml; Action: encourage fluid intake to above 1500ml, keep monitoring and Inform Shift Coordinator
  • 2 - Between 600ml and 1200ml; Action: encourage hourly fluids immediately, Inform Shift Coordinator
  • 3 - 600ml or less; Action: Inform Medical Staff Immediately
